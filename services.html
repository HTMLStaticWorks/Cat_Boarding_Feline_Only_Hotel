Pre-Boarding Assessment Program

To ensure your cat is placed in the ideal environment, I organize a comprehensive pre-boarding check-in assessment. I evaluate their response to heights, scratching surfaces, and baseline sound levels, creating an individualized comfort log before they stay. This allows me to pre-adjust their suite layout with the perfect climbing steps and toys.

On-Call Clinical Escalation Sync

Your cat's safety is my highest priority. I maintain formal emergency partnerships with the city's premier 24/7 veterinary triage hospitals. All treatment parameters sync automatically with your primary vet's database.
