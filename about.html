My Feline Care Journey

Learn about my professional background and why I built a quiet, cats-only boutique hotel.

Elena Rostova, Professional Feline Behaviorist

For over eight years, I worked as a veterinary care nurse and private feline behavior consultant. During my years in standard pet clinics, I constantly witnessed the immense stress cats experience when placed in boarding facilities that also house dogs. The sound of barking, the scent of predators, and the lack of vertical safety zones caused cats to stop eating, hide in fear, and display elevated stress indicators.

I decided to change this. I designed and constructed Whisker Suites as a specialized feline sanctuary. Every choice—from the soundproof partitions between suites to the vertical step climbing boards and private litter drawers—has been chosen by me to respect feline psychology. I do not run a franchise; I personally care for every single cat boarding in my hotel, managing their diets, games, and medical logs.

My Care Philosophy & Standard of Love

To me, cat boarding is not about cages and quick feedings. I believe every cat is an individual with specific environmental needs. My care philosophy is built around minimizing stress, maximizing comfort, and respecting territory boundaries. I work closely with owners to understand their cat's vocalizations, play behaviors, and dietary needs before boarding.

Veterinary Nursing & Behavior Education

Feline behavior sciences are constantly evolving. I prioritize continuing education to maintain standard-setting care protocols. I hold certifications in low-stress clinic handling, feline CPR/First Aid, and advanced feline nutrition, updating my credentials yearly to offer clinical safety for senior or high-needs boarders.

My Strict Sterilization Pledge

Pathogen containment is vital in multi-feline boarding. I keep an absolute cleaning log. My suites are double-sterilized between stays using clinic-grade, non-irritating veterinary disinfectants. All food bowls, grooming brushes, and toys are autoclaved individually, ensuring zero cross-contamination risk.

My Strict Comfort & Safety Protocols

Because I manage a boutique hotel, I enforce cleanliness standards that exceed average commercial boards. I sterilize every suite with medical-grade, cat-safe disinfectants between stays and run premium air purification systems to eliminate pathogens and airborne allergens.

  • HEPA-grade multi-filter air scrubbers in every room
  • Strict visual isolation to prevent territorial threat responses
  • Double-door safety entries to prevent any escape attempts
