Architectural Design for Visual Privacy

In feline care, preventing direct line-of-sight exposure between unfamiliar cats is the single most effective way to eliminate threat reactions. My facility is designed with custom visual partitions and angled entries. This guarantees that when your cat explores their climbing shelves or eats, they are completely shielded from the view of other guests.
