Senior & Medical Boarding Care Rates

Cats requiring daily veterinary supervision, insulin administration, or intensive senior care need dedicated attention. I offer supportive pricing packages for medical stays exceeding 10 nights. These packages include detailed medication logs, regular hydration checks, and specialized raw diet prepping, ensuring your senior cat gets premium clinical care at an accessible rate.

Flexible Booking & Refund Policies

Travel plans can change unexpectedly. I maintain transparent and flexible cancellation guidelines to accommodate schedule adjustments. Bookings cancelled up to 72 hours before arrival receive a full deposit refund. Within 72 hours, deposits can be converted into boarding credits for future bookings, ensuring no loss of investment.
