Standard-setting Veterinary Partnerships

I maintain a formal alliance with leading local feline veterinary specialists. In the rare case of any health issue, an experienced vet is immediately consulted, and my facility is fully equipped to handle medical needs such as insulin injections and chronic treatments.

  • 24/7 on-call veterinary specialist consultation

Specialized Feline Behavior Consultation

Felines are highly sensitive to environmental transitions. As a professional behaviorist, I conduct a complimentary behavioral assessment for every new guest upon arrival. I monitor their vocalizations, posture, and hiding indices, adapting their suite's pheromone levels and vertical accessibility to match their individual confidence profile.

Explore Behavior Care

A Balanced Daily Activity Schedule

Every day is structured around feline biological rhythms. Morning starts with checking hydration levels and veterinary diet prep, followed by personal interactive playtime (feather rods, laser chases). Mid-day is dedicated to calm sleep and grooming brushing. Evening wraps up with fresh litter preparation and a comforting vet check log update.
