Suite Specifications

Detailed dimensions, comfort elements, and vet-supervised security systems in my suites.

Crafted for Instinctual Security

Cats require elevated spots to feel safe and secure when observing new environments. I have custom-built every suite with vertical play steps, allowing guests to climb up to a cozy observation nest that is insulated from other suites.

Additionally, a dedicated veterinary monitoring program ensures that I check vital signs, hydration states, and feeding levels twice daily, logging all metrics for your peace of mind.

  • Multi-level solid wood construction (no wire cages)
  • Separate ventilation filters for individual suites
  • Private litter drawers with odor-absorbing carbon filters

Bring Their Favorite Comfort Items

Familiar smells make cats adapt much faster. I encourage you to bring your cat's favorite fleece blanket, scratching pads, or soft toys. Having objects saturated with home scents in their suite helps lower stress markers and prevents territorial confusion during their first few nights.

Detailed Medication & Chronic Care Logs

Managing medical needs requires absolute clinical precision. I chart all chronic treatments, pill administration, and insulin injections. Every dosage is logged in a transparent digital care journal, detailing times, quantities, and cat vitals, giving you secure updates throughout their boarding period.

View Medical FAQs

HEPA Air Filtration & Odor Control

Air hygiene is crucial to keep cats healthy. My facility features custom ducting configurations that circulate fresh, HEPA-filtered air through individual suites. This ensures that guest spaces have a dedicated supply of clean, odor-free, and allergen-free air, eliminating airborne pathogen circulation.
